ABOUT THE EVENT

THE GOAL

This charity padel tournament is dedicated to supporting the 93rd Brigade of Ukraine, whose story began in 2014 with the beginning of Russia's invasion of Ukraine.

All proceeds from this tournament will go toward purchasing evacuation buggies for the 93rd Brigade — helping accelerate the recovery and evacuation of wounded servicemen from the battlefield.

WHO WE ARE

Waiting for you in Cool Padel!

UKRAINA NIMEL MTÜ

We are an Estonian nonprofit created by Ukrainians and Estonians to provide direct help to the brigades of Ukrainian Armed Forces. Our goal is to unite the support from Estonian citizens, residents, and expats who stand against the Russian invasion of Ukraine - and convert it into actionable help for Ukrainian soldiers.

We believe it's most effective to PREVENT Ukrainian children from becoming orphans, families from becoming refugees, and civilians from dying or becoming disabled in the first place. And the only force able to stop this is the Armed Forces of Ukraine.

WHAT WE'RE RAISING FOR

The buggy we delivered last year, destroyed by Russian drone in Summer 2025

EVACUATION BUGGIES

We're raising funds to purchase evacuation buggies for the 93rd Brigade of Ukraine. These vehicles are crucial for safely evacuating wounded soldiers from the battlefield, providing immediate medical assistance and transport to field hospitals.

Each buggy costs approximately €15,000 (plus delivery and additional expenses) and can save countless lives. Your company's participation directly contributes to this life-saving mission.
